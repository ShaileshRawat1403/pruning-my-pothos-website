<p class="lead">A ritual is a gentle reset button, not a superstition.</p>

Calibration can be as simple as the first five minutes: one page read, one line written, one deep breath. The goal is not productivity theater; it is <span class="highlight">orientation</span>.

Rituals keep the day from owning you. They remind you what matters before the noise arrives. The mind does not need control, it needs a <span class="highlight">steady starting point</span>.
