<p class="lead">A good no is a yes to something more honest.</p>

Saying no is not rejection; it is <span class="highlight">focus with a spine</span>. It tells your time where it matters and tells others what you can actually sustain.

The craft is in the tone. Be clear, brief, and respectful. A clean no invites a better yes later.
