<p class="lead">Consistency is built on slack, not pressure.</p>

If your plan has no margin, life will cancel it. A small buffer is not laziness; it is a <span class="highlight">design for reality</span>.

Leave time for interruptions, energy dips, and the unexpected. What survives the margin becomes a habit, not a miracle.
