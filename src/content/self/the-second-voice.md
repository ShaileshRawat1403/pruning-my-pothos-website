<p class="lead">The second voice is the one that asks, "Are we sure?"</p>

It is calmer than the first and less impressed by urgency. The second voice is where <span class="highlight">discernment</span> lives.

Invite it on purpose. Pause, write down the first idea, then ask what it missed. This small ritual prevents many loud regrets.
